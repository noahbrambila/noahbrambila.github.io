Noah's Story

Late 2024: Noah first got involved in his community when he ran for ASB. After losing by a narrow margin, he decided to continue his involvement in the school by joining Community Service Club.

Early 2025: When the chance to run again, Noah took it. He talked with voters, asked them questions, and found valuable information he otherwise would not have.

24/25 2nd ASB Election: Noah won his election, becoming an ASB Representative. He contributed to 2 dances, the schools 25th anniversary, along with other social events and projects.

Late 2025: Noah, now with his term finished, decides to run again. He hears feedback from his peers, and they point to a few critical issues.

25/26 1st ASB Election: Noah won his election, becoming a two-time ASB Representative.

NOW:

Noah is running again because he knows the value of experience, dedication, and student engagement.
