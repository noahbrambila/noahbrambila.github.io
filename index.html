Meet Rep. Noah Brambila

Two-Time ASB Representative Noah has served in our Student Body for over a year now, and is planning to continue dedicating himself to his fellow peers. When running, voters have felt confident in his ability to represent them effectively. While in ASB, he has contributed to school-wide 4 dances, our schools 25th anniversary, and many other projects that helped our community and students. While in Community Service Club, he has helped create, plan, and execute ideas. After his latest semester term in ASB, he has decided to run again to represent his grade and fellow scholars.

Noah is a confident leader, planner, and do-er, always ready to take on the next challenge.
